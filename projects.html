Custom Translation Module

2022-2023 | Experteer

At Experteer, we relied on in-person translators because standard i18n translations often failed to capture the nuances of European languages. Our existing translation system was designed exclusively for Rails applications, but as we expanded to newer projects built with React.js and Next.js, we needed a way to integrate them without overhauling the old system.

To solve this, I built a custom translation application in Node.js that acted as a bridge between the old and new systems. The application automated the entire translation process in multiple steps: pulling updated files from GitLab, converting JSON files into a format compatible with the existing translation system, and pushing them for translation. Once translated, it retrieved the updated texts, merged them with the existing translations, converted them back to JSON, and pushed them to the GitLab repository.

This solution allowed us to leverage the existing translation workflow while seamlessly supporting modern projects, ensuring consistency across all platforms without disrupting the established process./p>

Key Achievements:

  • Built a scalable translation workflow to support multiple languages and regional variations while integrating with the existing Rails-based system.
  • Automated translation processing to optimize file conversions, updates, and synchronization, reducing manual effort.
  • Integrated with the legacy translation system to enable seamless multilingual support for modern React.js and Next.js projects.
  • Optimized performance by streamlining translation handling, ensuring efficient processing and reduced turnaround times.
  • Maintained cost efficiency by leveraging the existing system without major modifications, minimizing development and operational overhead.

Impact:

The custom translation module transformed how we handled multilingual content by seamlessly integrating modern React.js and Next.js projects with our existing Rails-based system. This solution expanded our platform’s global reach, ensuring accurate translations with minimal manual effort. It enabled efficient localized content delivery, improving the experience for international users while simplifying the editorial team’s workflow. By automating the translation process, we reduced operational overhead and ensured consistency across all supported languages..

One-Click Apply for candidates

2020-2022 | Experteer

At Experteer, I worked on one of the most impactful features—One-Click Apply—which completely streamlined the job application process. We noticed that many potential candidates dropped off due to a lengthy onboarding process before they could even apply for a job. To solve this, I built a backend system that allowed users to apply instantly without any friction, while still ensuring recruiters received a complete candidate profile.

Behind the scenes, as soon as a candidate clicked "Apply," my system took over. It automatically created an account and generated a profile based on their resume, eliminating the need for manual onboarding. To enhance their chances of landing a job, I integrated TalentInc, sending their resume for evaluation and potential improvements. At the same time, the system triggered CRM workflows to ensure smooth candidate engagement and published Kafka messages for tracking every step of the process.

To maintain a strong employer brand, I developed a mechanism to generate a new resume with Experteer branding, including our logo, header, and footer. This branded resume was then used to create and submit the job application on behalf of the candidate, ensuring recruiters and headhunters received all necessary details without any extra effort from the user.

Beyond just sending applications, I also implemented automated follow-ups—the system would check in with both the candidate and recruiter after a few days, keeping engagement high and improving the chances of a successful hire. To measure success, every action in the process was tracked with Google Analytics and Kafka events, allowing us to continuously optimize the flow.

I led the entire backend development of this feature, collaborating closely with the UX/frontend team and product managers to create a frictionless experience. This project not only improved the application process for candidates but also helped recruiters connect with actively interested job seekers more efficiently.

Key Achievements:

  • Implemented the full backend system for the One-Click Apply process.
  • Automated candidate onboarding, reducing friction in the application process.
  • Integrated TalentInc for resume enhancement, improving candidate presentation.
  • Enabled seamless recruiter interactions by automating application submissions and follow-ups.
  • Implemented real-time tracking with Kafka and Google Analytics for data insights.
  • Coordinated across teams, including UX/frontend and product managers, to deliver a streamlined experience.

Impact:

The One-Click Apply feature significantly improved sign-up rates, profile completion, and platform engagement. Candidates could apply for jobs instantly, making the process effortless and reducing drop-offs. Recruiters and headhunters benefited from immediate access to active candidates, improving their hiring efficiency. The automated follow-ups increased engagement, ensuring better communication between candidates and recruiters. By tracking every step with Kafka and Google Analytics, we gained valuable insights to further optimize the hiring funnel. Ultimately, this project boosted both candidate conversions and revenue for Experteer.
